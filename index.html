QUESTIONS

Before you pay ₹49, read this.

Will my friends and family have to pay to call me?

No. Anyone in your contacts — and anyone you allow — rings through normally, every time. Only unknown numbers are gated behind the ₹14 stake.

What exactly happens to my ₹49?

It reserves your founder spot today, and the full ₹49 is credited to your CallerPe balance when the app launches. If CallerPe doesn't launch, every founder deposit is refunded.

Is ₹49 the same as the ₹14 caller stake?

No. ₹49 is your founder deposit. ₹14 is the refundable stake that unknown callers put up when they want to reach you after launch.

Is CallerPe live today?

Not yet. This is the founder waitlist for the first Android rollout. Early members help validate demand before we open the app to a wider batch.

Who decides if a call was spam?

You do. The refund is the default — the caller automatically gets their ₹14 back unless you mark the call as spam. Genuine callers never lose money.

When do I get access?

We're rolling out in batches, founders first. You'll get a message on the number you registered when your batch opens. Follow our Instagram for progress updates.

What do you do with my number?

Waitlist and early access only. We don't sell your data, and nobody can pay us to reach you — that's the whole point of CallerPe.
